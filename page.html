This page will show you how to use MILP-CONDOR
 Inputs which are essential:
 Peptide sequence file or peptide sequence
 Protein Type

when you choose "Glob" you should provide both "Helix information" and "Strand information";

when you choose "Mem" you only need to provide "Helix information";
when you choose "Unknown", which means you don't know the type of the protein and we will predict the type of the protein for you.
